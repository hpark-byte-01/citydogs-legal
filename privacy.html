Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

CityDogs ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address, display name, and any profile photos you upload. If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive basic profile information from those services.

Phone Number

You may optionally provide your phone number in your profile. Your phone number is only shared with another user after both parties have confirmed a Dog Walk or Dog Sitting arrangement through the app. You can add or remove your phone number from your profile at any time.

Dog Profile Information

You may provide information about your dogs, including their name, breed, age, weight, temperament, and photos. This information is shared with other users to facilitate connections.

Location Data

With your permission, we collect your location to display nearby dogs and users on the map, calculate bark distances, and enable location-based features. You can disable location access at any time through your device settings.

Messages

Messages you send through the app (including direct messages and bark group chats) are stored on our servers (Supabase) to deliver them to other users and maintain your conversation history.

Push Notification Tokens

If you enable push notifications, we store your device's push notification token to send you alerts about new barks, messages, and bark responses.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the CityDogs service
  • Display your profile and dog information to other users
  • Show nearby dogs and users on the map
  • Deliver messages between users
  • Send push notifications for relevant activity
  • Improve and optimize the app experience

3. Information Sharing

Your profile information, dog profiles, and barks are visible to other CityDogs users. We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share information with service providers who help us operate the app (e.g., Supabase for data storage, Expo for push notifications).

Phone Number Sharing

If you add a phone number to your profile, it will only become visible to another user after both parties confirm a Dog Walk or Dog Sitting arrangement. Phone numbers are never displayed publicly or shared with other users outside of confirmed service arrangements. CityDogs does not verify phone numbers.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase's infrastructure. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, but no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Update or delete your profile information at any time
  • Add or remove your phone number from your profile at any time
  • Delete your account through the app settings
  • Disable location services through your device settings
  • Opt out of push notifications through your device settings

6. Account Deletion & Data Retention

When you request account deletion, your account is scheduled for permanent deletion after a 21-day grace period. During this period, your profile, messages, and conversation history remain intact to protect other users who may have relied on your services (e.g., dog walking or dog sitting). You can cancel the deletion at any time by logging back in. After 21 days, all your data is permanently deleted from our servers.

7. Children's Privacy

CityDogs is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
